About GreenSteps

A community-first platform for sustainable living

What you can do

  • Discover practices by category, effort & tags
  • Like and save your favorites
  • Share your own habits with the community
  • Get live weather-based eco tips

Built with care

  • No tracking, no ads
  • Works in any modern browser
  • SQLite in-browser database
  • Live weather via Open-Meteo API

Privacy-first

All data stays in your browser via sql.js (WebAssembly SQLite). No server required. Your account and practices are stored locally and never leave your device.
